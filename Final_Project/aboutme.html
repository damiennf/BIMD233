Hi, my name is Damien Flutre,
and I am currently 19. I attend the University of Washington Bothell campus, where I am studying computer science. I also currently live in Bothell. Although I have lived in the Seattle area for 12 years now, I was originally born in Montfermeil, France, which is near Paris. I speak French and I am proud of my nationality. My hobbies include exercising, gaming, reading, listening to music, and cooking. Exercise is a big part of my life, and I make sure to exercise every day to keep myself feeling good.



I am passionate about cars and the stock market, but I am most passionate about pattern recognition and problem solving. The stock market is an amazing way to learn and practice pattern recognition, and I feel mentally challenged when I analyze it, which is what initially drew me in. This also applies to coding and is one of the defining reasons I chose to become a software engineer. Coding allows me to flex my mental muscles and solve complex problems, which keeps me engaged. I also deeply enjoy learning about new topics, and make it a point to indulge in a new topic which I know very little about every month. This can range from learning about physics engines in coding, to learning about different languages and history.



I tend to think of myself as a balanced and reserved person. I tend to stick to routines, as I think that having a routine every day allows me to focus more mental energy on more enjoyable tasks such as exercising, stock market analysis, coding, and gaming. Although I tend to keep my life private, I am not closed off, and I love talking to new people and getting to know them.
